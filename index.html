The Investment

The $800,000 in capital from the EB-5 investor is fully at-risk and will be deployed into specific, concrete business activities. This section details the allocation of funds, demonstrating how the investment directly fuels the company's launch and job creation efforts.

Allocation of EB-5 Capital ($800,000)

The investment is strategically allocated to cover all essential startup costs for the first 24-36 months. A significant portion, **50% ($400,000)**, is dedicated to **Personnel Costs**, directly funding the salaries and benefits for the 10 new U.S. jobs. Other key allocations include **Office Lease & Fit-Out (15%)** to establish the physical headquarters, **Technology & Infrastructure (11.9%)** for essential hardware and software, and a **Working Capital & Contingency Reserve (9.4%)** to ensure financial stability during the initial growth phase.

The Impact: U.S. Job Creation

A core pillar of the EB-5 program is the creation of U.S. jobs. Proofsmith is committed to creating at least 10 full-time, permanent W-2 positions for qualified U.S. workers within two years, providing stable, well-paying employment in a TEA.
